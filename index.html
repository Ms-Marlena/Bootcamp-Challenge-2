About Me

Marlena Hooker Moore hails from King George County, Virginia. If you've never heard of it, that's ok. If you have heard of it, are you sure you aren't mixing it up with Prince George's County? Marlena trained as a musician and happily taught and performed music in the metro Denver area until she had a child of her own, whereupon she realized that there was something to be said for the reliability of a "day job." She then decided to do a whole career re-set and jumped into web development. Marlena figured that learning full-stack in its entirety would help her to choose the area for which she is best suited. Wish her luck!
